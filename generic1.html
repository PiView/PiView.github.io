Ease it dump in comfort

This project was part of makethon 1.0 2015-16 organised by Ink Talks. My team and I had absolutely no idea what we could possibly pitch when we entered the room for competition, then we come up with this crazy idea of redesigning the Western Toilet! Everyone was super excited and din’t look back thereafter. Our zest continued during makethon and gained momentum when we won it, HURRAY! Here is document explaining the flow from ideation to prototyping and finally building ease it – dump in comfort.

It was more important to learn through the process rather than to arrive at a solution. Through this project I was able to experience human centred design process that starts with the people you are designing for and ends with different and new solutions that are tailor made to suit their needs.
